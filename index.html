Glossary

  • Sports: whether online sports betting is legal.
  • Online: whether remote betting is allowed vs retail-only/state-run.
  • iGaming: whether online casino/poker is legal (or pending).
  • Tax %: effective operator tax (Tennessee uses handle tax).
  • Handle Tier: relative annual handle grouping (T1/T2/T3).

Methodology: rates and tiers are simplified for strategy storytelling, not statutory legal advice.
