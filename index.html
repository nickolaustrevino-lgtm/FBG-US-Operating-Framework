Glossary

  • Sports betting legal?: jurisdiction-level legality status for online sports betting. “Limited” typically indicates monopoly, tribal-only, retail-only, or otherwise constrained operator access.
  • Online/mobile?: whether customers can register and bet remotely. Notes such as “state-run only” or “Hard Rock only” signal restricted competitive availability.
  • iGaming: legal status for online casino/poker. “Pending” means law exists or is moving, but launch or licensing is not yet fully live.
  • Fanatics Sportsbook / Casino: current operating footprint. “Live” means Fanatics product is active; “Not live” indicates either whitespace or non-participation today.
  • Tax % (effective / simplified): high-level strategy rate for market comparison. Some states use tiered schedules, revenue-share structures, or non-GGR constructs (for example, Tennessee's handle tax).
  • Handle Tier: internal FBG grouping based on rolling 12M online handle (T1 largest, T2 mid, T3 long-tail); thresholds are directional and refreshed with new reporting.
  • Addressable handle share KPI: internal modeled share of total online handle represented by states in view where Fanatics is live or a plausible near-term target.
  • Live footprint KPI: Fanatics live states in view divided by legal-online states in view, highlighting penetration and whitespace.
  • Casino live footprint KPI: Fanatics Casino live states in view divided by live iGaming states in view, separating active footprint from pending iGaming regimes.

Methodology: rates and tiers are simplified for strategy storytelling; exact statutory tiers, revenue-share structures, surcharges, and offer restrictions should be validated with finance/legal.
